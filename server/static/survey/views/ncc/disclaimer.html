How Will My Responses Be Used?

Thank you for taking this survey! Your responses will help to build a cost-effective, partnerships-based marine protected area (MPA) monitoring program in the North Central Coast. Here are a few important things to know:

  • Most of the information you share with us through this survey will be publicly-accessible in the North Central Coast Survey Dashboard on OceanSpaces.org, available in Fall 2015. This will give you and others a chance to explore survey results.
  • Responses that will not be included in the public dashboard are indicated by the ‘Not Public’ icon:
  • We may share any of your responses with our state partners and funders as part of the process of building an MPA monitoring program in the North Central Coast.

Please contact Dina Liebowitz at dina.liebowitz@oceansciencetrust.org with any questions about the purpose of the survey responses and MPA monitoring in the North Central Coast.
